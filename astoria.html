Astoria, New York

  • Astoria is one of the neighrboods in Queens (one of the 5 boros of NYC).
  • Astoria is only a 15 minute subway ride from Manhattan!
  • One of the early settlers of Astoria in the 19th century was Heinrich Engelhard Steinway, who was the founder of the piano company Steinway & Sons.
  • In the 1960s, the majority of Astoria's population was Greek, making it known as a "Greek neighborhood" to this day.
  • The Steinways built one of their first piano factories in Astoria
  • Astoria is the home of Kaufman Astoria Studios, where many big-name movies and TV shows were filmed, including Men in Black III, Sesame Street, and Orange is the New Black
